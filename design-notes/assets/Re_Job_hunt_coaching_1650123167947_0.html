Example question: What is your life story? 

Try answering using the Pixar storytelling framework: "Once upon a time there was [blank]. Every day, [blank]. One day [blank]. Because of that, [blank]. Until finally [bank]." 

Example answer: Once upon a time I came from a poor family, my dream was to have an office job which my parents never had, I got to go to college which was such an achievement, then something happened, another thing happened, but despite these setbacks I studied programming on my own, you can see evidence of my skill on github and youtube, and then this saving thing happened, and due to that now I am here talking to you and ready to work, it is my lifelong dream to work at a good company like this one.

People love fairy tales so present them with one!
